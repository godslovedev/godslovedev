About Godslovedev

Godslovedev is a dynamic tech-driven brand specializing in web development and digital solutions. We provide innovative training and development services tailored for individuals and institutions...

Our expertise spans comprehensive computer literacy, computer-based testing (CBT) training, and hands-on web development programs. We are also passionate about visual arts — offering detailed pencil artwork that showcases creativity and realism.

Beyond training and tech, Godslovedev supplies quality electronics including projectors, laptops, and digital accessories. What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized learning — we enroll only a select number of students to ensure focused, in-depth mentorship and professional growth.

About The Founder

Hello! I’m Godswill Ogbonna, but you can call me Godslove. I’m a passionate frontend web developer, pencil artist, and a digital skills trainer from Abia State, Nigeria.

With a background in Physics and years of experience managing an electronics shop, I blend creativity and logic to build beautiful and functional websites. I also teach beginners—from art to basic computer skills to full web development—through my personal training programs.

I believe in creating with purpose, sharing knowledge, and making tech accessible to everyone.
